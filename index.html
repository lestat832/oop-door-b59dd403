About Out of Pocket

Out of Pocket is a free gesture-based reflex game for iOS. Players respond to rapid-fire prompts by tapping, swiping, shaking, pinching and tilting their phone across 14 unique gestures. The game features 5 modes: Classic for pure reflex speed, Memory for pattern recall, Stroop for cognitive challenges, Tilt for motion-based gameplay and PvP for head-to-head competition on a single device.
