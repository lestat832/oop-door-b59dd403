Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 12, 2026

Introduction

Get Out of Pocket ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Out of Pocket: Phone Gone Wild mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, including gameplay statistics, session duration, and feature usage
  • Crash Data: Technical information about app crashes to help us improve stability

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., when contacting support).

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the App experience
  • Display advertisements
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's Privacy Policy.

On iOS devices, you may be asked to allow or deny app tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If you deny tracking, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant to your interests.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This helps us improve gameplay and fix issues. Firebase may collect usage data and device information. You can learn more at Firebase Privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Google: For advertising (AdMob) and analytics (Firebase) purposes
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by law. Analytics data is typically retained according to Google's standard retention policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Choices

You have the following options regarding your data:

  • Ad Tracking: Use iOS Settings to manage App Tracking Transparency preferences
  • Personalized Ads: Opt out via your device's advertising settings
  • Analytics: You may limit analytics collection through your device settings
  • Delete Data: Contact us to request deletion of any data we hold about you

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
