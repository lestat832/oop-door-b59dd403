Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 12, 2026

Introduction

Get Out of Pocket ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Out of Pocket: Phone Gone Wild mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our App.

Information Collection

Information We Collect Directly

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our own servers. The App does not require account creation, login, or registration.

All gameplay data and settings are stored locally on your device only and are not accessible to us.

Information Collected by Third Parties

The App integrates third-party services that may collect information independently:

  • Google AdMob: Collects device identifiers and usage data to serve advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects usage data and device information to help us understand how the App is used

These services operate under Google's privacy policies. We do not have direct access to the data they collect.

How Information Is Used

  • By Us: We do not collect or use your personal information
  • By Third Parties: Google uses data collected through AdMob and Firebase for advertising and analytics purposes as described in Google's Privacy Policy

Advertising

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve personalized ads.

On iOS, you may be prompted to allow or deny tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If you deny tracking, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant to your interests.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand general usage patterns and improve the App. Firebase collects aggregated, anonymized usage data. Learn more at Firebase Privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not have access to personal data to share.

Third-party services (AdMob, Firebase) may share data as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Storage and Retention

Local Data

All App data (gameplay progress, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device. This data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

Third-Party Data

Data collected by Google (AdMob, Firebase) is retained according to Google's data retention policies. We do not control this data.

Your Choices and Rights

Local Data

You can delete all locally stored App data at any time by uninstalling the App from your device.

Third-Party Data

For data collected by Google services, you may:

  • Manage ad personalization at Google Ad Settings
  • Review Google's data practices at Google Privacy Policy
  • Request data deletion directly from Google per their policies

We cannot delete data held by third-party services on your behalf.

Device Controls

  • Ad Tracking: Manage App Tracking Transparency in iOS Settings
  • Personalized Ads: Adjust via your device's advertising settings
  • Analytics: Limit ad tracking in your device privacy settings

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through third-party services, please contact Google directly.

Security

We do not store your data on our servers. Local data on your device is protected by your device's security features. Third-party services implement their own security measures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@getoutofpocket.com

For questions about data collected by Google services, please contact Google directly through their support channels.
